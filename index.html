Budgeting Dashboard 1.5 👋

This app helps simplify you calculate and track your finances. Click next to see a step-by-step guide.

Step 1

Enter your net-monthly pay and any additional income.

Step 2

Enter any future income changes, increase or decrease, and any ad-hoc income for a specific month.

Step 3

Add your fixed (rent, car payments, etc..) and variable (loan repayments, subscriptions, etc..) costs.

Step 4

If you want to see projections for a specific month to include credit card statements, add them and select the statement month.

Step 5

If you want to save the dashboard inputs, give it a name and click "Save Scenario".

Step 6 🎉

Click run and see the calculations and projections, this can be adjusted for monthly/yearly outlook.
