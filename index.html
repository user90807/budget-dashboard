How to use the Budgeting Dashboard

Input values on the dashboard are net-monthly for income and monthly for pay (this is for the calculations).

Once all your values are correct, you can save it under "Scenarios".

When your inputs are correct, click run and the dashboard will show you the budgeting results- this can be changed to present yearly/monthly breakdowns.
